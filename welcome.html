Our compagny values

Omnicorps was created in XX23 by our government, the founders of North Turtle Island. Before, North Turtle Island was at the mercy of the South Turtle Island since all major online services such as social networking, online shopping, film & television streaming, videogame platforms, were provided through the internet by tech companies based in the neighboring country. But our government took initiative and took Omnicorps, that use to be a conglomerate monopoly and turned it into the company we are today!

Here at Omnicorps, we respect and believe in protecting user's data and privacy so that it may not be sold to other compagnies for profit. Our electronic wastes are properly disposed of instead of being sent to other countries illegally would also be important.Our Internet services is able to reach and include the whole turtle Island despite what their economic background may be. We think this is important because the Internet has become an important human resource and has become necessary for work or school.
